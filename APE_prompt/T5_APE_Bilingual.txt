(Style): Traditional Yangliuqing woodblock polychrome printing. Character outlines are vigorous, resembling "iron-wire" line drawing. Bamboo shadows are cast through blurred, carved window lattices. Dancing butterflies and falling pagoda tree flowers form a dynamic use of negative space.

(Camera): A macro lens captures the fleeting moment of water droplets rolling off lotus petals, then rapidly pulls back to a panoramic view. High frame rate and slow motion enhance the rhythm of flowing water and fluttering sleeves.
