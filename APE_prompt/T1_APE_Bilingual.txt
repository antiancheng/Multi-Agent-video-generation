T1：抱鱼娃娃抱着鱼转了个圈

（主体）一个杨柳青年画风格的抱鱼娃娃，圆脸大眼、红袄绿裤，头戴虎头帽，憨态可掬；怀中紧抱一条金鳞红尾的大鲤鱼，鱼身饱满，鳞片闪烁微光。  
（动作）娃娃原地欢快地转了个圈，衣袂随旋飞扬，脚尖轻点地面，脸上笑容灿烂；怀中鲤鱼甩尾摆鳍，溅起几滴晶莹水珠，在空中划出弧线。  
（风格）采用传统木版套印与手绘结合技法，色彩浓烈明快——朱红、翠绿、金黄为主调，线条流畅圆润，面部运用“开脸”细腻勾描，脸颊施以“三白法”提亮，整体充满吉祥喜庆的民俗韵味。  
（环境）背景为虚化的年节庭院，隐约可见红灯笼与梅花枝，地面铺着青砖，光影柔和，烘托出团圆祥和的氛围。  
（镜头）中景跟拍旋转动作，镜头随娃娃转动微微环绕，突出动态与喜悦感；结尾定格于娃娃正面笑脸，鲤鱼跃动，画面定格如年画成帧。

T1：The child holding a carp spins in a circle while holding the fish.

(Subject): A Bao Yu Wa Wa (child holding a fish) in the style of Yangliuqing woodblock prints, featuring a round face, large eyes, a red jacket, and green trousers. Wearing a tiger-head hat and appearing naive and cute. The child tightly embraces a large carp with golden scales and a red tail; the fish has a plump body and its scales shimmer with faint light.

(Action): The child joyfully spins in place; the hem of the clothes flies with the rotation, and the toes lightly tap the ground with a brilliant smile on the face. The carp in the arms thrashes its tail and fins, splashing a few crystal water droplets that trace arcs in the air.

(Style): Utilizing traditional woodblock polychrome printing combined with hand-painting techniques. The colors are intense and bright—primarily vermilion, emerald green, and golden yellow. Lines are smooth and rounded. The face features delicate Kailian (facial painting) tracing, with the Sanbai (Three Whites) technique used to brighten the cheeks. The overall aesthetic is filled with auspicious and festive folk charm.

(Environment): The background is a blurred Lunar New Year courtyard, with red lanterns and plum blossom branches faintly visible. The ground is paved with blue bricks, and the lighting is soft, evoking an atmosphere of reunion and harmony.

(Camera): A medium shot tracking the spinning motion; the camera circles slightly as the child rotates to highlight the movement and joy. It ends with a freeze-frame of the child’s smiling face and the jumping carp, with the final frame set like a traditional New Year print.
