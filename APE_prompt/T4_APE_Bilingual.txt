（主体）一个圆脸福娃身着红底金线绣牡丹的肚兜，头扎双髻缀珊瑚珠，眉眼用杨柳青年画典型“开脸”技法勾勒——三白法点染额头、鼻梁、下颏，怀中紧抱一尾鳞光灼灼的朱砂红金鱼，鱼眼圆睁如墨玉嵌银。  
（动作）金鱼奋力扭身甩尾，鱼鳍如绸缎绷直，鱼嘴大张似要喷出水珠，娃娃双臂急收却挡不住鱼尾拍打溅起的水花，几片金鳞在挣脱瞬间炸起，水珠飞溅成弧线折射七彩光斑。  
（风格）杨柳青木版年画经典构图，手工雕版线条刚劲流畅，矿物颜料厚涂呈现朱砂、石青、泥金的饱和古雅色阶，套印错位保留0.5mm以内传统工艺痕迹，鱼鳞用蛤粉堆叠出浮雕质感。  
（环境）背景为洒金宣纸肌理，左侧斜出太湖石透雕纹样，右侧垂落缠枝莲纹锦缎帷帐，地面散落两枚铜钱与半开的石榴，远处虚化处隐现桃花枝桠，整体留白遵循"疏可走马密不透风"年画布局法则。  
（镜头）慢速环绕推近镜头从娃娃惊愕表情特写拉开至全景，4K分辨率捕捉鱼尾甩动时水珠悬停的动态细节，柔光布光强化矿物颜料的丝绒反光，景深虚化背景突出主体冲突，丁达尔效应光束穿透飞溅水雾。

T4：The goldfish attempts to break free from the child’s embrace.

(Subject): A round-faced Fu Wa (fortune child) wearing a red bellyband embroidered with gold-threaded peonies. The twin hair buns are adorned with coral beads. The facial features are outlined using the classic Yangliuqing Kailian (facial painting) technique—specifically the Sanbai (Three Whites) method to highlight the forehead, bridge of the nose, and chin. The child tightly embraces a cinnabar-red goldfish with scorching scales and wide, ink-jade eyes inlaid with silver.

(Action): The goldfish thrashes its body and tail with all its might, its fins stretching taut like silk. Its mouth opens wide as if to spit out water. The child's arms tighten urgently, but they cannot stop the water splashes from the thrashing tail. Several golden scales flare up at the moment of struggle, and water droplets spray in arcs, refracting rainbow-colored spots.

(Style): A classic Yangliuqing woodblock print composition. The hand-carved lines are vigorous and smooth. Thickly applied mineral pigments present a saturated and elegant gradient of cinnabar, mineral blue, and liquid gold. Registration misalignments are kept within 0.5mm, preserving traces of traditional craftsmanship. The fish scales use gefen (clam powder) stacking to create a relief-like texture.

(Environment): The background features the texture of gold-flecked Xuan paper. On the left, a Taihu stone with openwork carving extends diagonally; on the right, a brocade curtain with interlocking lotus patterns hangs down. Two copper coins and a half-opened pomegranate are scattered on the ground. In the blurred distance, peach blossom branches faintly appear. The overall negative space follows the New Year print layout rule: "loose enough for a horse to run, yet tight enough to block the wind."

(Camera): A slow orbiting camera pulls back from a close-up of the child's astonished expression to a panoramic view. 4K resolution captures the dynamic detail of suspended water droplets as the tail thrashes. Soft lighting enhances the velvet-like reflections of the mineral pigments. A shallow depth of field blurs the background to highlight the central conflict, with Tyndall effect light beams piercing through the splashing mist.
