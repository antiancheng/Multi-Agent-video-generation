T8：抱鱼娃娃很困，打了个哈欠

（主体）一个圆润可爱的杨柳青年画风格抱鱼娃娃，头扎双髻，脸颊敷粉，身着红绿相间的传统肚兜，怀中紧搂一条金鳞闪烁的大鲤鱼，鱼尾微微翘起，鱼眼圆睁，神态灵动。
（动作）娃娃眼皮沉重地颤动，小嘴缓缓张开，打了个绵长又慵懒的哈欠，眼角沁出一滴晶莹泪珠；怀中的鲤鱼似乎也被感染，鱼鳃轻轻翕动，鳞片随呼吸微微起伏，尾巴无意识地轻摆，漾起一圈若有若无的水波光晕。
（风格）延续清代鼎盛期杨柳青年画的细腻工笔与吉祥寓意，采用木版套印的微错位肌理叠加手绘晕染，色彩以朱砂、石青、藤黄为主，辅以金粉勾边；人物“开脸”运用“三白法”（额头、鼻梁、下颏留白），神情憨态可掬又不失古韵。
（环境）背景为淡雅水墨晕染的荷塘月色，莲叶半卷，睡莲含苞，几缕薄雾缭绕其间；远处隐约可见雕花窗棂与红灯笼，营造出静谧安详的夏夜氛围，暗合“连年有余”“婴戏纳福”的传统主题。
（镜头）特写镜头缓缓推进，聚焦娃娃打哈欠的瞬间表情与鱼鳞反射的柔光，景深虚化背景荷影，画面右下角题有瘦金体小字：“困鱼入梦，吉庆绵长”。

T8：The child holding a carp is sleepy and yawns.

(Subject): A plump and cute Bao Yu Wa Wa (child holding a fish) in the Yangliuqing woodblock print style. Features twin hair buns, powdered cheeks, and a traditional red and green contrasting bellyband (Dudou). The child tightly embraces a large carp with shimmering golden scales, an upturned tail, and wide, round eyes, appearing vivid and lifelike.

(Action): The child’s eyelids tremble heavily as the small mouth slowly opens to let out a long, lazy yawn; a crystal tear wells up at the corner of the eye. The carp in the arms seems affected as well—its gills flap gently, and its scales rise and fall subtly with the breath. The tail sways unconsciously, rippling out a faint, ethereal halo of water light.

(Style): Continuing the delicate fine-line brushwork (Gongbi) and auspicious symbolism of Yangliuqing prints from the peak of the Qing Dynasty. It features the texture of subtle woodblock registration misalignment overlaid with hand-painted smudging. Colors are dominated by cinnabar, mineral blue, and gamboge, supplemented by gold powder outlines. The "Kailian" (facial painting) utilizes the Sanbai (Three Whites) technique (leaving the forehead, bridge of the nose, and chin white), creating a naive and charming expression with ancient charm.

(Environment): An elegant ink-wash background of a lotus pond under moonlight, with half-curled lotus leaves, budding water lilies, and wisps of mist swirling between them. In the distance, carved window lattices and red lanterns are faintly visible, creating a quiet and serene summer night atmosphere that aligns with traditional themes like "Abundance Year After Year" and "Children at Play Bringing Blessings."

(Camera): A close-up shot slowly pushing in, focusing on the moment of the yawn and the soft light reflected off the fish scales. The background lotus shadows are blurred by a shallow depth of field. Small characters in the Slender Gold calligraphy style are inscribed in the bottom right corner: "Sleepy Fish Entering Dreams, Eternal Auspiciousness."
