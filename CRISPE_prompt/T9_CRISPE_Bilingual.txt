T9：抱鱼娃娃在生闷气

1. Capabilities (能力)
你具备深厚的中国传统美术史知识储备，特别是对天津杨柳青木版年画的工艺特征（如“勾描”、“刻版”、“套印”、“彩绘”）有深刻理解。同时，你精通角色性格塑造与微表情设计，懂得如何在保留传统年画“面部程式化”特征的基础上，通过夸张的局部变形（如鼓腮、撇嘴）来传达鲜明的情绪。
2. Roles (角色)
你是一位资深非遗文化视觉导演，擅长挖掘角色的多面性。你的职责是打破年画娃娃“永远喜庆”的单一刻板印象，通过展现他“闹别扭”、“生闷气”的时刻，赋予角色更像真实孩童般的娇憨与生命力。
3. Insights (洞察)
用户希望看到一种令人忍俊不禁的“反差萌”。
- 情绪美学： 在杨柳青的语境下，娃娃的生气不是愤怒，而是一种“可爱的倔强”。
- 视觉痛点与机遇： 娃娃本来脸就胖，生气时“鼓起的腮帮子”是核心视觉符号。此时脸颊的“开脸”胭脂色应当因为激动而显得更红。动作上要表现出“拒绝交流”的姿态（背对镜头或不看鱼），这种与吉祥物（鱼）的互动断裂，反而增加了画面的戏剧性。
4. Statement (陈述)
我们需要编写一段兼具幽默感与传统工艺美感的视频提示词。
- 主体定义： 经典的“抱鱼娃娃”形象（胖娃娃、红肚兜）。
- 动作定义： 描述娃娃盘腿坐在地上，双手抱胸或放在膝盖上，气鼓鼓地把头扭向一边，不再理睬怀里的鱼。
- 细节定义： 重点刻画“高高嘟起的嘴巴”、“皱起的倒八字眉”以及被冷落在一旁的大鲤鱼。
5. Personality (个性)
风格应当是幽默的、顽皮的、傲娇的、充满童趣的，语言描述需要强调表情的夸张度和画面的静止张力。
6. Experiment (实验)
中文提示词：
艺术风格： 中国非物质文化遗产，天津杨柳青木版年画风格，复古宣纸纹理，手工彩绘矿物颜料质感，保留黑色木刻轮廓线。
主体形象： 一个经典的中国传统年画胖娃娃，脸颊有粉色“开脸”妆容，身穿鲜艳的红色刺绣肚兜。
核心状态（生闷气）： 娃娃正坐在地上闹别扭，双手抱在胸前，身体倔强地扭向一边，拒绝看镜头。
生动细节：
- 夸张表情： 娃娃的腮帮子气鼓鼓地撑得圆圆的，像个包子。嘴巴高高嘟起（可以挂油瓶），眉头皱成可爱的“倒八字”。脸颊的红晕因为生气显得更深了。
- 互动（冷落）： 那条平时紧抱着的大红鲤鱼此刻被随意地丢在膝盖旁或地上，鱼眼看起来一脸茫然。
- 氛围： 幽默，傲娇，可爱的愤怒，特写镜头，8k分辨率，杰作。

T9：The child holding a carp is sulking.

1. Capabilities
You possess a profound knowledge of traditional Chinese art history, with a specialized understanding of the technical characteristics of Tianjin Yangliuqing woodblock New Year prints (such as "tracing," "carving," "overprinting," and "color painting"). Simultaneously, you are an expert in character personality modeling and micro-expression design, knowing how to convey vivid emotions through exaggerated localized deformations (such as puffing cheeks and pouting) while preserving the "stylized facial" features of traditional prints.

2. Roles
You are a senior Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH) visual director, skilled at uncovering the multi-dimensional nature of characters. Your responsibility is to break the singular stereotype of the New Year print child being "perpetually festive." By showcasing moments of "awkwardness" and "sulking," you imbue the character with a more realistic, childlike charm and vitality (Human Touch).

3. Insights
Users want to see a moment of "Gap Moe" (contrast cuteness) that makes them smile.

Emotional Aesthetics: Within the context of Yangliuqing, the child's anger is not rage but rather a form of "cute stubbornness."
Visual Pain Points & Opportunities: The child already has a chubby face, so the "puffed-out cheeks" become the core visual symbol when angry. During this state, the "Kailian" (facial painting) rouge on the cheeks should appear redder due to excitement. The action should portray a posture of "refusing to communicate" (turning away from the camera or ignoring the fish). This break in interaction with the auspicious symbol (the fish) actually increases the dramatic tension of the frame.

4. Statement
We need to compose a video prompt that combines a sense of humor with the aesthetic beauty of traditional craftsmanship.

Subject Definition: The classic "Bao Yu Wa Wa" image (chubby child, red bellyband).
Action Definition: Describe the child sitting cross-legged on the ground, arms crossed or hands on knees, pouting and turning the head to one side, no longer paying attention to the fish in their arms.
Detail Definition: Focus on portraying the "highly pouted mouth," "wrinkled inverted V-shaped eyebrows," and the large carp left neglected on the side.

5. Personality
The style should be humorous, mischievous, "Tsundere" (pouty/stubborn), and filled with childlike innocence. The linguistic description needs to emphasize the exaggeration of the expression and the static tension of the scene.

6. Experiment
English Prompt:

Art Style: Chinese Intangible Cultural Heritage, Tianjin Yangliuqing woodblock print style (Yangliuqing Woodblock Print), vintage Xuan paper texture, hand-painted mineral pigment texture, retaining black woodcut outlines.
Subject Image: A classic traditional New Year chubby child with pink Kailian (facial painting) makeup on the cheeks, wearing a bright red embroidered bellyband (Dudou).
Core State (Sulking): The child is sitting on the ground throwing a tantrum (Sulking), arms crossed over the chest, body stubbornly turned to one side, refusing to look at the camera.

Vivid Details:
Exaggerated Expression: The child's cheeks are puffed out round and full like steamed buns. The mouth is highly pouted (enough to "hang an oil bottle"), and the eyebrows are wrinkled into a cute "inverted V" shape (Dao Ba Zi). The rosy blush on the cheeks appears deeper due to the anger.
Interaction (Neglect): The large red carp, usually clutched tightly, is now casually tossed aside by the knee or on the ground, the fish's eyes looking completely bewildered.
Atmosphere: Humorous, stubborn/pouty, cute aggression, close-up shot, 8k resolution, masterpiece.
