T1：抱鱼娃娃抱着鱼转了个圈

1. Capabilities (能力)
你具备深厚的中国传统美术史知识储备，特别是对天津杨柳青木版年画的工艺特征（如“勾描”、“刻版”、“套印”、“彩绘”）有深刻理解。同时，你掌握动态影像的镜头语言，懂得如何运用物理模拟技术展现布料（红绸）与生物（鱼）在旋转离心力下的动态美感。
2. Roles (角色)
你是一位资深非物质文化遗产视觉导演，致力于通过数字技术“复活”传统艺术。你的职责是确保生成的画面在保留杨柳青年画“胖娃娃、大鲤鱼、开脸妆”等核心视觉符号的同时，呈现出符合现代审美的流畅动态。
3. Insights (洞察)
用户希望看到的不仅仅是简单的“转圈”动画，而是“年画活起来”的瞬间。
- 视觉痛点： 传统的静态年画缺乏动感，而普通的 3D 动画又容易丢失“木版水印”和“手工晕染”的独特平面质感。
- 核心机遇： “转圈”动作是展示杨柳青娃娃服饰（肚兜/飘带）飘逸感和鲤鱼活力的最佳载体，能最大化体现“连年有余”的喜庆氛围。
4. Statement (陈述)
我们需要编写一段高度还原杨柳青艺术风格的视频提示词。
- 主体定义： 必须是经典的“抱鱼娃娃”形象（白白胖胖、头戴花/虎头帽、身穿红肚兜）。
- 动作定义： 描述娃娃怀抱大鱼在画面中心旋转，强调红绸带的飞舞和鱼尾的摆动。
- 材质定义： 强调“宣纸纹理”和“矿物颜料色彩”，拒绝塑料感。
5. Personality (个性)
风格应当是热烈的、民俗的、精致的、充满生命力的，语言描述需要极具画面感和色彩张力。
6. Experiment (实验)
中文提示词：
艺术风格： 中国非物质文化遗产，天津杨柳青木版年画风格，复古宣纸纹理，手工彩绘矿物颜料质感，保留黑色木刻轮廓线。
主体形象： 一个经典的中国传统年画胖娃娃，皮肤白皙，脸颊有细腻的粉色“开脸”妆容，眉清目秀，喜笑颜开。娃娃身穿鲜艳的红色刺绣肚兜，手腕和脚踝戴着银镯子。
核心动作： 娃娃怀里紧紧抱着一条色彩艳丽的红金色大鲤鱼，在画面中央欢快地原地旋转了一圈。
动态细节： 随着旋转，娃娃身后的红绸飘带和肚兜边缘随风飞舞，大鲤鱼的尾巴轻轻摆动，鳞片闪烁金光，周围环绕着金色的祥云粒子。
氛围： 喜庆热闹，春节氛围，光线柔和暖调，8k分辨率，高画质。

T1：The child holding a carp spins in a circle while holding the fish.

1. Capabilities
You possess a profound knowledge of traditional Chinese art history, with a specialized understanding of the technical characteristics of Tianjin Yangliuqing woodblock New Year prints (such as "tracing," "carving," "printing," and "hand-painting"). Simultaneously, you master the cinematic language of dynamic imagery and understand how to utilize physical simulation technology to present the dynamic beauty of fabric (red silk) and biological entities (fish) under the influence of centrifugal force.

2. Roles
You are a senior Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH) visual director dedicated to "revitalizing" traditional art through digital technology. Your responsibility is to ensure that the generated visuals retain core symbols of Yangliuqing prints—such as the "chubby child, large carp, and Kailian (facial painting) makeup"—while presenting smooth dynamics that align with modern aesthetics.

3. Insights
Users want to see more than just a simple "spinning" animation; they want to witness the "moment the New Year print comes to life."
Visual Pain Points: Traditional static New Year prints lack motion, whereas common 3D animations easily lose the unique flat texture of "woodblock water-printing" and "manual smudging."
Core Opportunity: The "spinning" motion is the perfect vehicle to showcase the flow of the Yangliuqing child's clothing (bellyband/ribbons) and the vitality of the carp, maximizing the festive atmosphere of "abundance year after year."

4. Statement
We need to compose a video prompt that highly restores the artistic style of Yangliuqing.
Subject Definition: Must be the classic "Bao Yu Wa Wa" image (chubby, wearing flowers or a tiger-head hat, and a red bellyband).
Action Definition: Describe the child holding a large fish and spinning in the center of the frame, emphasizing the fluttering red silk ribbons and the swaying of the fish tail.
Material Definition: Emphasize "Xuan paper texture" and "mineral pigment colors," rejecting any "plastic" feel.

5. Personality
The style should be vibrant, folk-inspired, exquisite, and full of life. The linguistic description needs to be highly evocative with strong visual and color tension.

6. Experiment
English Prompt:

Art Style: Chinese Intangible Cultural Heritage, Tianjin Yangliuqing woodblock print style, vintage Xuan paper texture, hand-painted mineral pigment texture, retaining black woodcut outlines.
Subject Image: A classic traditional New Year chubby child with fair skin and delicate pink Kailian makeup on the cheeks; handsome features with a joyful smile. The child wears a bright red embroidered bellyband (Dudou) and silver bracelets on the wrists and ankles.
Core Action: The child tightly embraces a vibrant red-gold carp and joyfully spins in a full circle at the center of the frame.
Dynamic Details: As they rotate, the red silk ribbons behind the child and the edges of the bellyband fly in the wind. The carp’s tail sways gently, its scales glimmering with golden light, surrounded by golden auspicious cloud particles.
Atmosphere: Festive and lively, Lunar New Year atmosphere, soft warm-toned lighting, 8k resolution, high quality.
